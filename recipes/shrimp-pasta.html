Shrimp Scampi with pasta

Description

Shrimp scampi with linguine is the ultimate seafood pasta dish. Works with any pasta.

This quick and easy shrimp scampi pasta recipe will be an instant favorite

Ingredients

  • Shrimp
  • Pasta
  • Butter
  • Shallots
  • White wine

Steps

  1. Fry shrimp and scampi
  2. Cook in white wine
  3. Boil pasta
  4. Mix together
