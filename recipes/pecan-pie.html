Pecan Pie upside down cake

Description

Classic pecan pie with a modern twist

A pecan pie, but upside down

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Sugar
  • Syrup
  • Pecan nuts
  • Cinnamon
  • Cake batter

Steps

  1. Mix all dry stuff
  2. Make cake batter
  3. Turn on oven
  4. Bake in the oven for 9 minutes at 175 degrees celsius
